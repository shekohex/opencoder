# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-04

## Summary

OpenCoder does not sell personal data. We collect only what is needed to operate the app.

## Data We Collect

- Account and authentication data provided by your Coder instance
- App diagnostics and crash logs (if enabled)
- Basic device and app information (OS version, app version)

## How We Use Data

- Authenticate you to your Coder workspace
- Provide core app functionality
- Improve reliability and performance

## Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except:

- When required by law
- To comply with your configured Coder server policies

## Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by your organization.

## Your Choices

- You can disable diagnostics in your device settings where applicable
- You can remove the app at any time

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the project maintainers via the repository issues page.
